Developed a four-wheel autonomous robot using Arduino that can detect and follow human movement in real time. This project demonstrates practical knowledge in embedded systems, sensor integration, and real-time control logic.

It demonstrates understanding of renewable energy concepts, system design, and innovative approaches to energy harvesting.
